French Toast

Description

French Toast is dish consisting of bread soaked in beaten eggs, milk as well as other additives before being pan-fried.

A satisfying start to one's day.

Servings: 4 servings


Ingredients

  • 4 1-inch thick slices of bread
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp butter for frying
  • 2 tbsp Maple Syrup

    • Optional

  • Powdered Sugar
  • Fresh fruit

Directions

  1. (Optional) Cut slices into halves or thirds
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla and cinnamon
  3. Dip slices in to the egg mixture
  4. Cook each side until golden brown
  5. Top with Maple Syrup and any other toppings of your choice

Nutritional Facts

Per Serving: 266 calories; 11.4g protein; 24.7g carbohydrates; 13.9g fat; 218.7mg cholesterol; 204.4mg sodium
