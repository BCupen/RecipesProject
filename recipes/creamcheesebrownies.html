Cream Cheese Brownies

Description

Cream Cheese Brownies! Perfect for any occasion. Guaranteed to satisfy your sweet tooth.


Servings: 20 servings

Ingredients

    Brownie Batter

  • 12 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup natural cocoa powder
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs + 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

    • Cheesecake Batter

  • 8oz cream cheese (room temperature)
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and spray a 7x11 pan with baking spray
  2. Combine butter and chocolate chips in a large bowl
  3. Microwave for 30 seconds. Stir well and microwave again for another 15 seconds. Repeat until chocolate and butter are completely melted and well combined
  4. Add cocoa powder and stir
  5. Add sugars, stir until completely combined
  6. Add eggs and then egg yolk, one at a time. Stirring well after each one
  7. Add vanilla extract and salt. Stir to combine
  8. Add flour and stir until completely combined
  9. Spread 3/4 brownie batter in prepared pan

    10. Cheesecake

  10. Combine cream cheese and sugar in a bowl
  11. Stir in egg and vanilla extract. Stir until well-combined
  12. Pour cheesecake over prepared bwonie batter
  13. Take remaing brownie batter and drop over cheesecake
  14. Use knife to swirl brownie batter and cheesecake
  15. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 25-30 minutes, until centre is set and toothpick inserted comes out clean
  16. Let cool completely before cutting and serving

Nutritional Facts

Per Serving: 232 calories; 2g protein; 26g carbohydrates; 13g fat; 55mg cholesterol; 107mg sodium; 19g sugar
